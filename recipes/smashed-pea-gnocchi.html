Smashed Pea Gnocchi

Description

A creamy and zesty one-pan pasta dish for the summer. By MOB kitchen, the original recipe can be found here.

Ingredients

  • 500g fresh gnocchi
  • 80g parmesan
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter
  • Bunch of basil
  • Black pepper
  • Salt
  • 160g frozen peas
  • 3 tbsp crème fraîche
  • Bunch of asparagus
  • Olive oil
  • Lemon

Steps

  1. Trim and slice the asparagus, and roughly chop the basil.
  2. Bring a large pot of water to a boil, season with salt and blanch the gnocchi (save a cup of cooking water) until they float to the top. Fish them out into a bowl and coat with a dash of oil. Blanch the asparagus and the peas for 1 min, drain and set aside.
  3. Now, heat a large frying pan over a high heat. Add a shot of oil and let that get nice and hot. Add the gnocchi and cook until golden brown. Keep the gnocchi moving regularly.
  4. Add the butter and cook until brown when the gnocchi is glazed and nut brown. Add some pasta water, the smashed peas and the crème fraîche. Toss everything together to create a beautifully green sauce.
  5. Season with salt and black pepper. Add lemon zest, parmesan, the blanched veg and chopped basil. Keep tossing everything together until glossy and gorgeous. Adjust the seasoning if you need to.
