Smashed Potato Salad

Description

An unexpected twist on the well-beloved potato salad with a creamy and tangy dressing. By Alfie Steiner, the original recipe can be found here.

Ingredients

  • 750g baby new potatoes
  • 3tbsp greek style yoghurt
  • 3tbsp mayonaise
  • 3tbsp cucumber
  • 2tbsp red onion
  • 2tbsp pickled gherkins
  • 1-2tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 garlic clove
  • Paprika
  • Pepper
  • Garlic powder
  • Olive oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Place your potatoes in a pot of cold, salted water. Bring to a boil, then simmer until fork tender (25-30 minutes). Meanwhile, preheat your oven to 200 degrees celsius.
  2. Make the dressing. In a mixing bowl, combine mayo, yogurt, lemon juice + garlic. Mince gherkins, cucumber, onion, chives + dill and add to bowl. Mix well, season to taste with salt and pepper. Refrigerate.
  3. Once the potatoes are fork tender, strain and let steam until dry. Then smash on a lined baking tray. Sprinkle with seasonings & olive oil.
  4. Roast (30-45 mins). Turn half-way through.
  5. Combine potatoes with dressing. Serve as a side or main.
