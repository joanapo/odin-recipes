Mediterranean Chicken Thighs

Description

A perfect main to have with smashed potato salad. Perfect for a summer lunch or dinner with some greek yoghurt on the side. By Aysegul Sanford, the original recipe can be found here.

Ingredients

  • 600g chicken thighs
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 cup bri’ish greek yogurt
  • ¼ cup lemon juice
  • Fresh parsley
  • Cumin
  • Paprika
  • Pepper
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Combine yogurt, lemon zest, lemon juice, olive oil, garlic, onion powder, cumin, oregano, paprika, parsley, and salt and pepper in a large bowl. Submerge chicken thighs in marinade.
  2. Cover and place in the fridge for at least 4 (1.5) hours or preferably overnight for up to 12 hours.
  3. Remove the chicken from the marinade (scrape off any excess marinade) and place on lined baking sheets.
  4. Bake for 15-20 minutes at 220°C fan, flipping over halfway to cook through.
